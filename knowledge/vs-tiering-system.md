# VS Tiering System

Purpose: give the judge a consistent scale for Attack Potency and Durability.

Core rule: higher tier normally wins the raw stat exchange unless the lower-tier character has a reliable hax, speed, range, immortality, regeneration, or battlefield-control win condition.

## Working order

- 9-C to 9-A: street to wall/small building level.
- 8-C to 8-A: building to multi-city block level.
- 7-C to 7-A: town to mountain level.
- 6-C to 6-A: island to continental level.
- 5-C to 5-A: moon to large planet level.
- 4-C to 4-A: star to multi-solar-system level.
- 3-C to 3-A: galaxy to universe level.
- Low 2-C: Universe level+.
- 2-C to 2-A: low multiverse to multiverse+.
- Low 1-C to High 1-B: complex multiverse to high hyperverse.
- 1-A and above: outerversal ranges.

Source reference: https://vsbattles.fandom.com/wiki/Tiering_System

## Judge guidance

- Use the character key being debated, not the strongest composite by default.
- If a profile has multiple tiers, pick the highest clearly applicable key and explain the assumption.
- "Possibly" and "likely" ratings lower confidence.
- Unknown stats cannot be used as a positive win condition.
- Do not upgrade a character based on vibes, popularity, or statements without profile support.
