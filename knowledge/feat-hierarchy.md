# Feat Hierarchy

The judge must prioritize evidence in this order:

1. Direct feats with clear scan, chapter, episode, or calc blog.
2. Explicit profile statistics from the applicable VS Wiki key.
3. Author or narrator statements that are consistent with feats.
4. Scaling chains where every link is named and sourced.
5. Inference from abilities or lore.

## Rules

- Feats beat statements when they conflict.
- Statements need context and cannot automatically override shown limits.
- Scaling is valid only when the relation is direct enough to defend.
- Outliers require stronger evidence than normal feats.
- A feat must answer what happened, who performed it, when it happened, and why it applies to this key.

## Output requirement

Every major claim must include a source link or state that the claim needs source support.
