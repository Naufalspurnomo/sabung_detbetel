# Scaling Rules

Scaling means Character A gets a rating because they compare to Character B.

## Valid scaling

- A directly harmed, blocked, overpowered, or survived B.
- A is officially stated equal/superior to B and the statement matches feats.
- A uses the same power source or form with clear comparable output.
- The profile itself lists the scaling chain.

## Invalid scaling

- "They fought once" without proving both were serious or comparable.
- Scaling across different continuities without composite rules.
- Scaling from a stronger form to a weaker form.
- Scaling from travel speed to combat speed.
- Scaling through vague titles such as "god", "strongest", "limitless", or "omnipotent".

## Judge language

If a user gives incomplete scaling, mark it partially supported and ask for the missing link.
