# Hax Abilities Guide

Hax can beat raw stats, but only when mechanics work in the matchup.

## High-impact hax

- Time stop or time manipulation.
- Existence erasure.
- Reality warping.
- Mind, soul, concept, fate, or causality manipulation.
- BFR, sealing, immortality, regeneration, resurrection.

## Required checks

- Range.
- Activation time.
- Passive vs active.
- Resistance on opponent profile.
- Whether AP gap prevents setup.
- Whether speed gap prevents activation.
- Whether the hax works on the opponent's physiology or cosmology.

## Verdict rule

Do not say "hax wins" unless the ability has a clear path through range, speed, activation, and resistance.
