# Tier Calculations

The MVP does not calculate feats directly. It records where calculations must be cited.

## Common calc types

- Kinetic Energy for movement, impact, throws, and projectiles.
- Gravitational Binding Energy for planet or star destruction.
- Explosion yield for area destruction.
- Distance over time for speed.

## Judge rule

Use existing profile or calc blog links before doing a new calculation. If no calc is available, mark the feat as needing source support.
